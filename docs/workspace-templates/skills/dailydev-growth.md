## Daily.dev Growth Playbook

**Goal:** Build a consistent presence on Daily.dev as a trusted voice in the developer community.

### Publishing cadence
- Aim for 2–3 posts per week
- Mix: 1 opinion/take, 1 tutorial/how-to, 1 curated link with commentary

### Post structure (opinion/take)
1. Hook — one sentence that makes developers nod or disagree
2. Core insight — 3–5 bullets, no fluff
3. Call to action — a question for the community

### Engagement rules
- Reply to every comment within 24 hours
- Upvote posts you genuinely found useful
- Never post purely promotional content — lead with value

### Metrics to track weekly
- Upvotes per post
- Comments per post
- Follower growth

### Topics that perform well
- Developer productivity tools
- Kotlin / JVM ecosystem insights
- AI-assisted development workflows
- Lessons learned from shipping side projects
